Teaching Experience

Graduate Teaching Assistant

I had a great experience serving as a Teaching Assistant for Undergraduate course Digital logic Design (CprE 281) in Fall 2017 and Spring 2018 semesters for a class consisting of more than 300 students. My responsibilities were as follows:

1) Conducting labs every week to help students write code in Verilog HDL
2) Helping students in their final course project on deploying applications on FPGA
3) Holding office hours to facilitate one-to-one interaction for resolving queries
4) Grading homeworks, lab reports, exams and providing appropriate feedback
5) Proctoring Midterm and Final exams
